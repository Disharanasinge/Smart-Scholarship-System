⚠️ Beware of Fake Scholarships

Fraudsters often create fake scholarship websites to collect personal information or money. SmartScholar helps you stay safe:

  • Only verified scholarships from trusted sources
  • Community-powered fraud reporting
  • Admin-reviewed trust scores
  • Direct links to official websites only
Never Pay to Apply for a Scholarship

Legitimate scholarships are free to apply for. If you're asked to pay, it's a scam. Report it immediately.
